Kewpie Mayo Ramen

Description

Kewpie Mayo Ramen Hack. An odd but delicious ramen recipe that is easy, simple and very creamy. It only requires instant ramen, the seasoning packet, egg, kewpie mayo, minced garlic and water. Ready in just 10 minutes and divine. You can use any instant ramen of your choice but Iâ€™m using the spicy ...

Ingredients

  • 1 Ramen Packet w/ seasoning packet
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of Kewpie Mayo
  • 1 tsp of soysauce
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • Optional: 1 tbsp of hot sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook ramen as instructed.
  2. Mix all other ingredients, including the seasoning packet
  3. Drain ramen onced cooked, and add ingredient slurry.
  4. Cook ramen with the mixture for one minute, or until no longer runny
  5. Enjoy!
