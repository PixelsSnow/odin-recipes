Angel Hair Garlic Pasta

Description

An incredibly simple pasta dish that has incredible flavor and can satisfy even the pickiest of eaters

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of Unsalted butter
  • 8 oz of angel hair pasta
  • 2 tablespoon of parsley
  • 2 tsp of lemon juice
  • salt and pepper, cracked
  • 1 cup of parmesean cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook angel hair pasta to desired state
  2. At the same time, melt butter in a pan at medium-low, (make sure the pan is large enough for the noodles)
  3. Once butter is melted, add together the garlic until golden brown
  4. Add the parsley and lemon juice, and mix together. Turn off heat
  5. Drain noodles, and add into the pan with the garlic & parsley. Add parmesean.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Enjoy!
